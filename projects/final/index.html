This website explores Asian LGBTQ cultural folktales, myths, legends, rituals and practices that are particular to our own personal cultures that we heard growing up, stories passed down from our ancestors, or just tales that we have researched out of curiosity. The stories we present range from descriptions of mythical creatures, to recounts of historic tales, cultural practices, and pure superstition.

& deconstruct

Through this project, we hope to invalidate existing heteronormative constructions in mythology. We also hope to create a platform to expose fascinating fictitious worlds that are hidden in much of Asia.
